Lasagna

Lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ½ lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 28oz crushed tomatoes
  • Two 15oz cans tomato sauce
  • 6oz tomato paste
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup parsley, chopped
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown beef & sausage; drain. Sauté onion & garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes, sauce, paste, herbs, salt & pepper. Simmer ≥ 30 min.
  3. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley.
  4. Cook noodles; drain.
  5. Layer: sauce, noodles, ½ ricotta mix, 1 cup mozzarella. Repeat. Top with sauce, noodles, 1 cup mozzarella.
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C), covered, 25 min. Uncover, bake 25 min more.
  7. Rest 10-15 min before serving.
